Game Design Ideas

Concept Ideas with mockups

Get ME!!!

A 2D interactive puzzle game where you have to get ingredients from kitchen drawers and give it to chef for food preparation. Give the proper ingredient in proper order else you'll mess up the dish!

Dismembered me

You committed a forbidden act and now are dismembered! But hey, you are alive! Travel this magical realm and find your lost limbs. Put yourself back together and get revenge from the punisher!

Dog game

Wanna adopt a dog but confused? A dog simulator where we can adopt and pet different breeds of dogs. The game highlights the importance of dogs how they are user friendly.

Utro bhai

A train crowd management game. "Are andar chalo Khali hai". A strategic 2D game where you have to manage people in an Mumbai Local train and make sure they get off at the proper station within time.

JOHN WICK

An FPP action shooter based on the world of the movie series John Wick. The player plays as John Wick, a retired assassin who has has set out for revenge as some gangsters kill his puppy and steal his car.

I be the Villain

An 3D adventure action-puzzle game where the player either plays as an Cursed Super-cop OR an 14 year old magical girl, daughter of late-gangster who aspires to be like her dad.
